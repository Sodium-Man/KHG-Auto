Warranty Overview

Protection designed for used vehicle ownership

As vehicles get older, components can be more likely to fail. An extended warranty option may help with eligible repair costs, including parts and labour, depending on the cover selected and applicable terms.

Available warranty periods shown in the brochure include 12, 36 or 60 months, with cover pathways designed for vehicles across different age and kilometre bands.

Cover Guide

Vehicle eligibility pathways

The guide presents five cover levels. Availability, suitability and pricing should be confirmed at enquiry stage.

Option A

Any vehicle, regardless of age and distance travelled.

Drive train limit: $1,000 Comprehensive component limit: $350

Option B

Vehicles 20 years and under that have travelled less than 280,000 km.

Drive train limit: $2,000 Comprehensive component limit: $750

Option C

Vehicles 15 years and under that have travelled less than 200,000 km.

Drive train limit: $3,000 Comprehensive component limit: $1,250

Option D

Vehicles 10 years and under that have travelled less than 160,000 km.

Drive train limit: $5,000 Comprehensive component limit: $1,500

Option E

Vehicles 8 years and under that have travelled less than 120,000 km.

Drive train limit: $7,000 Comprehensive component limit: $2,500
